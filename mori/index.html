Interests

  • Information theory: Low-density parity-check codes, belief propagation, graph covers, polar codes.
  • Computer science: Constraint satisfaction problems, fully polynomial-time approximation scheme for #P-complete problems, holographic transformation, quantum computation.
  • Statistical physics: The Bethe approximation, cluster expansions, partition function zeros, replica method.
